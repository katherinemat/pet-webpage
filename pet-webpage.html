MEET REX

This is REX. He is an angler fish.

REX was found on a fishing trip in the Atlantic Ocean south of England

Angler fish are ancient fish that live thousands of feet under water and have a light hanging from their head.

REX's favorite activities

  • Tricking little fish with his light
  • Chasing Marlin and Dory
  • Using his strong sense of smell to hunt prey

Facts about his species

  • Uses bacteria to illuminate it's bulb
  • Ranges from eight inches to three feet long, and can weigh up to 110 pounds.
  • It is a carnivorous fish
  • Can swallow prey twice their size
